Xenotune – AI-Powered Music Generator

Xenotune is an intelligent platform that generates infinite ambient music in modes like Focus, Relax, and Sleep. Powered by FastAPI and Flutter, it combines deep learning, MIDI-to-MP3 conversion, and seamless loop integration for real-time generation.

The system uses LSTM models to compose melodies and harmonizes them with layered instrumentation, configurable via JSON. Real-time MIDI conversion with audio blending allows uninterrupted, immersive experiences for users.

Xenotune brings code and creativity together. It’s not just music generation, it’s adaptive audio evolution.
