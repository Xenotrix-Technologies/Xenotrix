Maximize Efficiency through Intelligent Automation

If your team repeats a digital process daily, it should be automated. Xenotrix deploys intelligent systems that handle data entry, generate scheduled reports, synchronize APIs, and orchestrate complex business workflows — so your workforce can focus on high-value strategy while code handles the operations.

Our Automation Services

Workflow Orchestration

Connecting fragmented business platforms (CRMs, payment processors, spreadsheets, and databases) using customized pipelines built with n8n, Apache Airflow, and custom FastAPI middleware.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Software bots that mimic human mouse clicks and keystrokes on legacy systems or web interfaces using Playwright, Selenium, and Python. Seamlessly scrape data, automate logins, and input high-volume records.

Asynchronous Job Processing

Decouple long-running operations from your user-facing applications. We configure resilient Celery, Redis queue, and RabbitMQ message systems to process heavy tasks silently in the background.

Automated Document Generation

Build automated engines to parse client records, generate invoices, create custom PDFs, and compile scheduled XLS dashboards. Directly email reports to your team or upload to secure storage buckets.
