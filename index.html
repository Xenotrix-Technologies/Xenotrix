What is Xenotrix Technologies?

Xenotrix Technologies is an innovation-driven company specializing in AI integration, data analytics, and digital solutions that empower businesses and individuals to achieve more through technology.

What services does Xenotrix offer?

We offer a wide range of services including AI-based project development, data analysis, web and mobile app development, and project center support for students and startups.

What is Xenotune?

Xenotune is our flagship AI-powered music generation platform built with FastAPI and Flutter. It creates infinite, mood-based background music using LSTM neural networks and sound synthesis technology.

How does Xenotrix support students and startups?

We provide hands-on project guidance, AI implementation training, and software development mentorship for academic and entrepreneurial projects, ensuring real-world learning and innovation.
